How to Use: Web App

The easiest way to use MOAL is with the web application here: https://belindabgarana.shinyapps.io/moal . With our web app, you can query our results across large-scale drug screens in adherent cancer cell lines to identify drug mechanisms-of-action (MOAs) which may be toxic to the same cell lines (NES > 0) or opposite cell lines (NES < 0). For more information, please Contact Us.

To avoid errors, please refresh your web browser after each query. Please note that you will also need to refresh your connection to the web app after 5 minutes of inactivity.

How to Use: R Package

To use the MOAL R package, please refer to our README.md, vignette, and man 'help' files on our GitHub repository. You can also refer to example scripts under our Results folder.
